# FileMaster Landing Page Redesign Instructions

## Objective

Completely rewrite and restructure the FileMaster marketing landing page.

The goal is NOT to explain the internal technology.

The goal is to maximize:

- Product clarity
- Perceived value
- Professional appearance
- Enterprise credibility
- Conversion rate
- Trial downloads
- Sales inquiries

The landing page should feel comparable to modern B2B SaaS products such as Stripe, Linear, Vercel, Notion, Retool, Figma, or Datadog.

---

## Product Context

FileMaster is a desktop application that helps organizations compare:

- Folders vs Excel spreadsheets
- Folders vs CSV files
- Folders vs JSON records
- Excel vs Excel
- Structured records vs real files

The software automatically detects:

- Missing files
- Extra files
- Mismatched records
- Data inconsistencies

It generates audit reports and helps organizations validate large datasets quickly.

Typical users include:

- Inventory teams
- Operations departments
- IT departments
- Compliance teams
- Auditors
- Records management teams
- Data migration teams

## Real-World Validation & Business Impact

FileMaster is not a prototype or concept product.

It is already being used in a real business environment to automate file reconciliation workflows that were previously performed manually.

Before FileMaster:

- Employees manually reviewed hundreds or thousands of files.
- Records were compared against master Excel spreadsheets by hand.
- Audits could take multiple days or even an entire work week.
- Human error was common and difficult to detect.

After FileMaster:

- The same reconciliation process is completed in seconds.
- Missing, extra, and matched files are identified automatically.
- Reports are generated instantly.
- Teams can focus on resolving issues rather than searching for them.

### Proven Business Impact

Real-world usage has demonstrated:

- Audit times reduced from days to seconds.
- Average savings of approximately $230 USD per week in labor costs.
- Significant reduction in repetitive manual work.
- Improved confidence in audit accuracy.
